Tips & Tricks

Alter Your Lifestyle

- Eat less meat or try alternatives such as fish, poultry, or plant-based meals from companies like Beyond Meat

- Choose organic and locally sourced

- Spend less on fast fashion and consider buying secondhand

- Use a reusable bag when shopping or bring your own

Transport and Energy

- If you have appliances over 10 years old, consider replacing them with Energy Star appliances.

- Consider other means of transportation for short distances such as walking or cycling

- Be concious of your fuel efficiency as it can help save 2 tons of carbon emissions yearly

- For longer trips, use cruise control to stabilie fuel consumption

Our Community

- Vote for policies that protect the environment or align with the views of the Environmental Protection Agency through the local elections for bills that are in consideration

- Speak to our representative Veronica Escobar to push more environmental laws in the government to be enforced or created

- Invest in recycling bins for recycling centers such as the El Paso Waste Managment & Recycling through placing products in blue bins marked by the recycling logo

- Switch to products that are composed of biodegrdable materials by companies such as "Allbirds" that use merino wool (The Guardian)
